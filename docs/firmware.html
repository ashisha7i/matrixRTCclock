PitStop 3D

WiFi Tetris Clock Installer

❗WARNING❗

Updating firmware improperly may brick your clock.
Proceed at your own risk.

Before you begin:
  • Connect your device to this computer via USB.
  • Use Chrome or Edge on desktop — mobile and Firefox are not supported.

Select the firmware variant you want and click Install.

Blink ON

The colon between hours and minutes blinks every second.

Blink OFF

The colon between hours and minutes stays on steady.

After clicking Install, a popup will ask you to select a serial port. Choose the one that corresponds to your device (the name will differ from the example below).
